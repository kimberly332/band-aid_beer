About Us


In 2010, we set out to brew beer with equal parts flavour and sessionability. During a decade, we have operated over 5 retail stores, served 20,000 licensed customers, and 62 retail partners. Today, Band-Aid Beer has a family of award-winning beers, a community of passionate fans, and a belief of never looking back.
